## THE 5 RULES THAT GOVERN ALL DECISIONS

1. **Etsy before own site.** Every hour spent on our site is an hour away from the channel that has buyers right now. Graduate traffic to our domain in Month 3+.

2. **The subscription is the business.** Every sale, email, and pin should end with a path to the Monthly Drop. It's the only product that makes revenue recurring.

3. **The Studio is the moat.** Feature it in every single ad, pin, email, and video. "Try free before you buy" removes the entire risk objection.

4. **Volume beats perfection in content.** One imperfect TikTok posted beats one perfect TikTok sitting in drafts forever. Post daily. Improve over time.

5. **Never lead copy with "we."** Every word of copy should speak directly to the customer. "Your tracker. Your habit. Your name." not "We make trackers."
