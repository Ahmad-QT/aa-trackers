## THE COPY SHIFT (Wiebe framework)

**The unspoken objection every visitor has:**
*"Why would I pay $11.97 when Pinterest has free printable grids?"*

Current copy: "makes consistency feel good" — emotional but doesn't differentiate.
Needed: Answer the objection directly.

**Hero headline A/B tests:**
1. *"The only habit tracker with a character that cheers you on — and a free studio to try before you buy"*
2. *"Free grids exist everywhere. None of them have a mochi, a couple mode, or a sticker row."*
3. *"Buy once. Print every month. Never feel guilty for a missed day again."*

**Copy rules for all content:**
- Lead with **"you"** not "we" — "Your tracker. Your name. Your habit."
- Lead with the **undated story** — "Miss a week? No blank squares judging you. Pick up whenever you're ready."
- **Apps vs print** — "Apps charge $9/month forever. This is $11.97 once. Print it every month, forever."
