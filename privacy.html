Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Adorable Accountability ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit adorableaccountability.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address — collected when you sign up for our free kit, newsletter, or Monthly Design Drop membership.
  • Payment information — processed securely via LemonSqueezy. We do not store your card details.
  • Order information — name, email, and delivery address (for physical orders only).
  • Analytics data — we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill your order and deliver your digital or physical products
  • To send you your free kit and the email series you opted into
  • To send monthly membership content if you're a Club member
  • To improve our website and marketing based on aggregate analytics

3. Email Marketing

If you provide your email, you consent to receiving emails from us via ConvertKit. Each email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

  • LemonSqueezy — payment processing. See their privacy policy at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy
  • ConvertKit — email marketing. See their privacy policy at convertkit.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics 4 — site analytics. You can opt out via browser extensions.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies via Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@adorableaccountability.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@adorableaccountability.com
