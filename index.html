Our Story

I wanted a habit tracker that didn't make me feel like I was failing.

Every productivity app felt like a performance review. Every blank planner page felt like evidence I wasn't doing enough. I didn't need more pressure — I needed something gentle.

So I made what I wished existed: trackers that feel like a warm hug, not a drill sergeant. Cute characters that cheer you on. Stickers that make your brain go "yes, more of that." Undated grids so a missed day doesn't mean a failed month.

This isn't about being perfect. It's about showing up — even imperfectly — and seeing the progress add up.

Adorable Accountability is for anyone who wants to build better habits without the intensity. One cute square at a time. 🌸
